Largo

Tips

  • Largo's Encore innate extended duration also applies to item buffs. This makes him the ideal hero on the team to be casting Smoke of Deceit.
    • Agh's Shard Encore innate also duplicates item buffs applied on allies, like Glimmer Cape, Lotus Orb, Linken's Sphere, or Urn/Vessel/Distiller.
    • Agh's Shard Encore can duplicate the HP regen buff from Catchy Lick onto an ally.
  • Many small jungle camp creeps apply debuffs on attacks. If you need some HP regen in lane from Catchy Lick's dispel, you can try Licking a lane creep you've pulled into the small camp that's tanking camp aggro. You can click any unit in the game to see what debuffs they're currently affected by.
  • Catchy Lick is a rare type of basic dispel that can be cast on enemies, allies, and Largo self-casting it on himself.
    • On enemies, some noteworthy dispellable buffs: Abaddon's Aphotic Shield, Dark Seer's Surge and Ion Shell, Ember Spirit's Flame Guard, Gyrocopter's Rocket Barrage, Monkey King's Jingu Mastery, Slardar's Guardian Sprint, Techies' Reactive Tazer, Ursa's Overpower, Windranger's Windrun, ethereal states like from Necrophos's Ghost Shroud or Ghost Scepter, many sources of barriers, and Haste/Amplify Damage/Arcane/Regen/Shield (but not Invis) runes.
    • On allies, some noteworthy dispellable debuffs: most silences, slows, fears, roots, and other non-stun debuffs like Bounty Hunter's Track, Slardar's Corrosive Haze, or Urn/Vessel/Distiller.
  • The forced movement from Catchy Lick is not a disable and will not cancel enemy channels, like TPs.
  • The forced movement from Catchy Lick on enemies can cancel spells like Magnus's Skewer, Phoenix's Icarus Dive, Spirit Breaker's Charge, Techies' Blast Off, and Templar Assassin's Meld.
  • Catchy Lick turns targets to face you. Could be useful against Bristleback or heroes that panic jump when in trouble like Mirana's Leap or Zeus's Heavenly Jump.
  • Units pulled by Catchy Lick have unobstructed pathing. They can be pulled up/down cliffs and don't destroy trees while traveling.
  • Catchy Lick cannot pull allies affected by Batrider's Flaming Lasso, Enigma's Black Hole, Faceless Void's Chronosphere, Legion Commander's Duel, or Primal Beast's Pulverize.
    • However, Pulverize can be cancelled if Primal Beast gets affected by a long distance of forced movement. So if you're trying to cancel Pulverize, Lick Primal from max range, not your ally.
  • You can pull targets through a Disruptor's Kinetic Field or out of Slark's Pounce leash with Catchy Lick.
    • Be aware that these interactions work whether they're allies or enemies. Don't grief your teammate Disruptor by Licking enemies out of his Kinetic Field. Although, you could help him by Licking an enemy into the Field.
  • You can cast Catchy Lick on runes to pull them. The mana cost on Lick even gets refunded.
  • If things are getting chaotic in a fight, know that you can target spells on allies like Catchy Lick or Croak of Genius by clicking on the allied hero's portrait in the top bar. You can't target spells on enemies using top bar portraits, however.
  • Frogstomp and Amphibian Rhapsody Bullbelly Blitz damage works fully on invisible and fogged enemies.
  • Any instances of spell damage that can interact with spell damage amp (like from Kaya) can proc the reverberation on Croak of Genius.
    • So things that won't reverberate include HP removal sources (like Necrophos's Heartstopper Aura), instant attacks (like Ember Spirit's Sleight of Fist or Gyrocopter's Flak Cannon), or no-reflection sources (like Blade Mail or Warlock's Fatal Bonds).
    • Some heroes don't spend mana on their spells like with Huskar's Burning Spears or Phoenix's Icarus Dive. These spells won't get their HP cost reduced, but that also means the Croak of Genius buff duration won't tick down faster on them either.
  • Croak of Genius will only apply the reverb debuff on enemies if the source of damage and the target are within 2000 range of each other.
  • Croak of Genius + Bloodthorn is a cute combination since all the extra damage from Bloodthorn's active is sourced to the owner of the Bloodthorn. Handy way to get reverb damage from attacks on an enemy instead of just spells.
  • You can cast Croak of Genius on creep-heroes. Really the only worthwhile ones are Brewmaster's Primal Split Fire Brewling or Warlock's Golem since other creep-heroes don't deal significant spell damage.
  • The timing on landing Amphibian Rhapsody beats is far more generous than the UI would have you believe. You don't have to wait for the beat to enter the marker circle, instead you can hit it pretty much as soon as the beat gets towards the end of the "neck" of the guitar on the UI. Here's a visual showing how early a valid beat can be hit.
    • There's no advantage to always strumming "early." Beats timings eventually average out no matter how early/late you're hitting the beats.
  • Bullbelly Blitz can add damage to attacks from allied illusions.
  • Don't hesitate to play a little Hotfeet Hustle when trying to move around the map or when getting from camp to camp while farming.

Counters

  • Catchy Lick turns targets to face Largo. So don't panic mash a Mirana's Leap or Zeus's Heavenly Jump if you get Licked or you may just launch yourself further out of position.
  • Croak of Genius can be dispelled off enemies with basic dispels, like from Eul's or Nullifier.
