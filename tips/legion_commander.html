Legion Commander

Tips

  • You always get the bonus attack speed from Overwhelming Odds even if you don't hit any enemy units.
  • Press the Attack is a strong dispel. You can cast it on allies to wipe off most disables and debuffs, including stuns.
  • Moment of Courage can only trigger if you're currently being attacked by a unit. If the count has hit zero, but no targets are attacking you, Moment of Courage won't activate.
  • It can be worth keeping neutral creeps alive with minimal HP to keep attacking you for more Moment of Courage procs.
  • If you have a Shadow Blade/Silver Edge, casting Duel will end the invisibility without proccing the item attack bonuses because casting spells ends invisibility.
  • Legion Commander can cast any of her spells while in Duel.
  • Unlike Axe's Berserker's Call and Winter Wyvern's Winter's Curse, Duel is a taunt that also applies a silence on the target. This means Ursa can't Agh's Scepter Enrage and Abaddon can't manually cast Borrowed Time while Dueled.
  • You do not earn Duel damage from Aegis deaths, Arc Warden's Tempest Double, or illusions.
    • You can earn Duel damage from Wraith King Reincarnation deaths, deaths that turn him into a Wraith from his Vampiric Spirit innate, and even dueling his Wraith form itself when it times out counts as a Duel victory.
    • In fact, with the "Duel Refreshes Cooldown on Victory" talent, you could Duel him, kill him, then Duel his Wraith form again for even more damage.
    • One last thing to note is if you're Dueling a target that will turn into a wraith, like either WK himself with his innate or an ally of WK with his Agh's Scepter, Legion Commander herself has to get the killing blow to get the Duel damage. If an ally gets the last hit, you won't get the Duel win.

Counters

  • Simple spells to mess with Duel: Dazzle's Shallow Grave, Oracle's False Promise, Winter Wyvern's Cold Embrace, Bane's Nightmare, Outworld Destroyer/Shadow Demon/Tusk banishes, Centaur Warrunner's Agh's Scepter Hitch A Ride, Slark's Agh's Shard Depth Shroud, Snapfire Agh's Scepter Gobble Up.
    • Some of these spells outright end the Duel and some of them will have Legion Commander eagerly waiting to attack the target once they emerge from the banish. There isn't really any logic.
    • Other ways to mess with Duel include Disarms (ex. Heaven's Halberd) or Ethereal states (ex. Ghost Scepter).
  • LC loves going against teams with a bunch of summons thanks to Overwhelming Odds damage scaling off number of units hit, so draft accordingly.
  • The barrier Legion Commander gets with her Agh's Shard Overwhelming Odds can be dispelled.
