Gyrocopter

Tips

  • You can use Force Staff on the Homing Missile for some reason.
  • Flak Cannon hits creeps if you're targeting a tower, but it won't hit the tower if you're attacking creeps.
  • Each Flak Cannon attack behaves like an independent right click in that they can miss from evasion, land from accuracy, or be reduced by damage block. However, Flak Cannon attacks will not carry item modifiers, only the primary target can be affected.
  • Fading into a Shadow Blade while attacking something with Flak Cannon activated will deal the backstab damage to all targets in the AoE while maintaining your invis. This only works for the one fading attack, though.
  • The Side Gunner provided by Gyro's Agh's will prioritize the furthest enemy unit within its attack range.

Counters

  • Taking on Gyrocopter 1v1 in the early or mid game is probably suicidal. Rocket Barrage deals a ton of magic damage to you if you eat all of it. Get a huddle going with your creeps if Gyro is trying to make a move on you.
  • You can dispel Rocket Barrage.
  • Creeps can't kill the rocket. While they draw aggro from it, only tower shots or hero attacks can damage it.
  • The Homing Missile can be Hurricane Piked and attacked at max speed.
