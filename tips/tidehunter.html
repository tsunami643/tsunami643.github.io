Tidehunter

Tips

  • Gush applies minus armor on enemies. Try to use it before Anchor Smash since Anchor Smash is physical damage.
  • Anchor Smash is an extremely easy spell to animation cancel (hit the stop key). This is mainly relevant if you're laning against another melee hero since you can bait them out of getting last hits by throwing out fake Smashes.
  • Anchor Smash's instant attack has true strike and can proc all items that a normal right click can, except cleave.
  • Since Anchor Smash includes a 'right click,' it'll break your Smoke if it damages a unit.
  • Anchor Smash works on ancient creeps, but not Roshan.
  • Agh's Shard Dead in the Water provides true sight and exposes targets who move into fog.
  • Agh's Shard Dead in the Water's fixed move speed even works on enemies who are hasted or have slow resistance.
  • Agh's Scepter Gush provides a small radius of vision around every affected unit for 2 seconds. It can also hit invisible enemies, but won't give true sight.

Counters

  • Kraken Shell can be disabled with Breaks, like from Silver Edge or Viper's Viper Strike.
  • Kraken Shell can dispel a lot of debuffs, but certain persistent aura based debuffs like Disruptor's Static Storm or unit based debuffs like Grimstroke's Phantom's Embrace can be difficult for Tide to deal with.
  • Get a Magic Stick in lane if Tide is gonna be spamming Anchor Smash.
  • Ravage has a travel time as it expands outward, so if you're quick, you can attempt Force Staffing or using some other repositioning spell/item to evade it.
    • You can even Blink inside of it since the spell AoE is a ring, not a filled circle.
