Wraith King

Tips

  • Wraithfire Blast is a slow projectile and the animation is extremely obvious. Use the stun as close to the enemy as you can to prevent people from disjointing it or running away. Or pump fake and cancel the cast animation to mess with enemies and bait defensive spells.
  • The "Reincarnation casts Wraithfire Blast" talent will only target enemy heroes. It will not target illusions or creep-heroes.
  • Casting Wraithfire Blast on an enemy will redirect your Bone Guard Skeletons to attack that enemy.
  • Bone Guard skeletons will always move to the closest attackable unit that's in vision. If there's nothing in vision, they'll head to the nearest lane and act like lane creeps. When jungling, try to get vision on the next camp you want to farm as the current camp is being finished off to keep your skellies on the same page.
    • Consider using your courier to get vision of the next camp to guide your skeletons to.
    • Skeletons won't block neutral creep camps.
  • Denying creeps adds to charges Bone Guard. Not denying Skeletons, though. There is no infinite Skeleton tech.
  • You can attack, use spells, use items, and have unobstructed pathing while in Wraith Form from your Vampiric Spirit innate.
    • Agh's Scepter Vampiric Spirit also gives allies all the same Wraith Form benefits.
  • Sometimes it's advisable to sit on the skill point for Reincarnation. There's no point putting the spell on cooldown early on if your team can't take advantage of it and you're going to die twice anyway, so decide to level it when you need it.
    • However, you need to skill Reincarnation before your Vampiric Spirit Wraith Form activates. Reincarnation cannot trigger out of Wraith Form.
  • Reincarnation triggers before Aegis does if you have both.
  • You can manually trigger Reincarnation by self-casting the spell. Useful if you think you're going to get mana burned or repositioned to a bad spot (like from a Disruptor Glimpse) before your HP drops to zero.
  • An emergency Mango can be handy to supplement mana needed to Reincarnate in the early game.

Counters

  • Your all-purpose mana burners can make it difficult for Wraith King to have mana for Reincarnation. Anti-Mage (Mana Break), Bane (Fiend's Grip), Invoker (EMP), Lion (Mana Drain), Nyx Assassin (Mana Burn innate), and Silencer (Glaives of Wisdom) are some choices. Heroes who easily build Diffusal Blade can also be useful for burning mana.
    • Once Wraith King maxes out Reincarnation, the mana cost goes to 0.
  • You can get Desolator stacks, Bloodseeker Sanguivore heal, Bounty Hunter Track gold, Legion Commander Duel damage, Lion Finger damage, Necrophos Heartstopper regen multiplication + permanent Reaper's Scythe buffs, and Underlord Atrophy Aura damage off of a Reincarnation death.
    • You do not get Urn/Vessel/Distiller charges, Silencer INT steals, Slark permanent Essence Shift steal, or Pudge Flesh Heaps.
    • Windranger's Focus Fire persists through Reincarnation.
    • The aforementioned interactions are consistent with generic Aegis deaths excluding Legion Commander Duel damage.
    • If Wraith King has an Agh's Scepter, these interactions are also true for him or his allies once the Wraith Delay expires because that's when they actually "die." Existing debuffs are maintained to an enemy's Wraith form, it's kinda like they're under a Dazzle's Shallow Grave.
