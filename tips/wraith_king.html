Wraith King

Tips

  • Wraithfire Blast is a slow projectile and the animation is extremely obvious. Use the stun as close to the enemy as you can to prevent people from disjointing it or running away. Or pump fake and cancel the cast animation to mess with enemies and bait defensive spells.
  • The "Reincarnation casts Wraithfire Blast" talent will only target enemy heroes. It will not target illusions or creep-heroes.
  • Vampiric Spirit skeletons can't be controlled, but all skeletons with 2000 range of your hero will converge on any target that was just hit with a Wraithfire Blast.
  • Skeletons will always move to the closest attackable unit that's in vision. If there's nothing in vision, they'll head to the nearest lane and act like lane creeps. When jungling, try to get vision on the next camp you want to farm as the current camp is being finished off to keep your skellies on the same page.
    • Consider using your courier to get vision of the next camp to guide your skeletons to.
  • Skeletons won't block neutral creep camps.
  • An emergency Mango can be handy to supplement mana needed to Reincarnate in the early game.
  • Sometimes it's advisable to sit on the skill point for Reincarnation. There's no point putting the spell on cooldown early on if your team can't take advantage of it, so decide to level it when you need it.
  • Reincarnation triggers first before Aegis does if you have both.
  • You can manually trigger Reincarnation by self-casting the spell. Useful if you think you're going to get mana burned or repositioned to a bad spot (like from a Disruptor Glimpse) before your HP drops to zero.
  • Agh's Scepter gives you and your allies free pathing when in Wraith Form.

Counters

  • Mana burns make WK's life hard. Heroes like Invoker (EMP), Nyx Assassin (Vendetta), Lion (Mana Drain), Silencer (Glaives of Wisdom), Clockwerk (Cog burn), Anti-Mage (Mana Break) are obvious counterpicks. Heroes who easily build Diffusal Blade (Phantom Lancer, Riki, Naga Siren, Spectre) are also good.
    • Wraith King can deal with mana burners by building Agh's Shard to make Reincarnation free.
  • You can get Desolator stacks, Axe Culling Blade buffs, Bloodseeker Thirst heal, Bounty Hunter Track gold, Legion Commander Duel damage, Lion Finger damage, Necrophos Heartstopper regen multiplication + permanent Reaper's Scythe buffs, and Underlord Atrophy Aura damage off of a Reincarnation death.
    • You do not get Urn charges, Silencer INT steals, Slark permanent Essence Shift steal, or Pudge Flesh Heaps.
    • The aforementioned interactions are true with generic Aegis deaths as well.
  • Windranger's Focus Fire persists through Reincarnation (including Aegis Reincarnation).
