Hoodwink

Tips

  • Acorn Shot trees give bonus regen when eaten by Tangoes, just like Iron Branch trees. However, enemies can also benefit from eating your tree the same way.
  • Acorn Shot trees give 200 ground vision.
  • Acorn Shot can bounce to units in fog.
  • Acorn Shot bounces can proc any attack modifiers your hero has. They also ignores disarms. However, the shots can high ground miss if you're on a lower terrain than your targets.
  • Even if Acorn Shot attacks miss, the slow still lands.
  • If you target Bushwack far enough away, you can shoot an Acorn Shot tree into the Bushwack AoE to beat the net projectile.
  • An Acorn Shot tree will never time out if targets are currently Bushwhacked to it.
  • Bushwack gives flying vision around its landing location even if it doesn't latch anything.
  • Bushwack can connect on fogged enemies, but not invisible enemies.
  • You have significantly more survivability when you're hugging trees. Pay attention to your buffs and try to keep Scurry enabled whenever possible.
  • Your Acorn Shot tree can enable Scurry evasion.
  • You can use the recoil from Sharpshooter to move up and down cliffs to escape. It works while rooted or leashed, allowing you to break out of things like Slark's Pounce. You can even get out of Disruptor's Kinetic Field or Mars's Arena.
  • Only Hoodwink's player gets vision from the Sharpshooter indicator. Allies can't see what you see, neither the vision given by the indicator nor the indicator itself.
  • Enemies can hear you winding Sharpshooter up even through fog.
  • You are not disabled during the Sharpshooter windup. You can't move or attack, but you can cast all your spells or items (except TP).
  • However, you are limited to targeting spells and items perfectly in the direction you're facing. If you target something outside of the angle allowed, nothing will happen until Sharpshooter ends. If you want to re-target a spell/item to fix that, issue a stop or hold command, then try targeting again.
  • Nothing can cancel Sharpshooter except the subspell or dying. Stuns, silences, banishes, doesn't matter, this squirrel's gonna get her nut.

Counters

  • Eating Acorn Shot trees with Tangoes give bonus regen, like with Iron Branch trees.
  • If you destroy the tree that heroes are Bushwhacked to, it'll immediately cancel the stun.
  • If you activate Spider Legs right before getting Bushwacked, you'll destroy the tree you move to and immediately free yourself.
  • Applying any type of forced movement to a Bushwhacked hero will also free them. Like from a Force Staff or Snapfire's Firesnap Cookie.
  • If a melee hero Lotus Orb reflects Acorn Shot, the reflected shots will only deal damage if the hero is within 350 + melee hero attack range (default 150) of the bounce targets. This is because Acorn Shot considers your hero as launching the bounce attack from their current location and melee heroes aren't allowed to hit targets more than 350 range away.
  • Nothing can stop Sharpshooter from going off so don't bother wasting spells trying to cancel it. Just try to juke the projectile.
  • You can dispel the Sharpshooter debuff with basic dispels.
