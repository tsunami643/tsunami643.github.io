Omniknight

Tips

  • Go for Purifications in lane whenever melee enemies are going in for last hits.
  • Purification can damage invis enemies. If you pay close attention, you can faintly see some diamond particles traveling to the invis target if you land it.
  • Heavenly Grace is a strong dispel. It can take stuns and most other debuffs off an ally when cast.
  • Targets that are banished/cycloned at the time of Guardian Angel's cast will not receive the buff.
  • Omniscience is great for diving. Guardian Angel will completely negate any damage from towers since they're physical damage.

Counters

  • Dispels like Invoker's Tornado, Brewmaster's Storm Brewling's Cyclone (and obviously Dispel Magic), Oracle's Fortune's End, Enchantress's Enchant, and Shadow Demon's Demonic Purge can remove Guardian Angel and Heavenly Grace off enemies. Nullifier or Eul's can also be bought as dispels.
  • Rubick's wet dream. "Oh I better cast something before Rubick steals Guardian Angel... but I don't want him getting Heavenly Grace. Wait, I don't want him getting Purification either..."
  • Spirit Vessel, Skadi, and Shiva's are useful items to reduce heals on enemies.
  • Bane's Enfeeble reduces health restoration. Ancient Apparition's Ice Blast completely prevents health restoration.
  • Revenant's Brooch turns right clicks into magic damage allowing you to penetrate Guardian Angel.
