Void Spirit

Tips

  • There is logic to how Aether Remnant chooses a target when there are multiple units in its AoE. Whichever target is closest in a perfect 0Â° line from the Remnant, it grabs first. If there are multiple targets that are perfectly lined up, it'll choose the one furthest away from the Remnant.
  • Casting Aether Remnant on an Aegis reincarnation will instantly stun the enemy when they respawn.
  • There is no way to prevent the forced movement of Aether Remnant. If your Remnant collides with a hero who's under another type of forced movement, Aether Remnant's pull will take priority.
  • If you Aether Remnant an enemy who's being Lasso'd by Batrider, it'll immediately cancel the Lasso.
  • Aether Remnant can drag heroes up and down cliffs.
  • Aether Remnant is a Hypnotize which also prevents enemies from activating spells. So Abaddon can't activate Borrowed Time manually if he's in a Remnant.
  • Aether Remnants provide flying vision over the "watch range" and the Remnant itself.
  • Aether Remnant pulls invisible and Smoked enemies. If you place Remnants on top of stairs in the late game, you may be able to detect some sneaky enemy movement.
  • Placing a Remnant on top of an important ally during a high ground siege could make it difficult for enemies to initiate on them.
  • Aether Remnant does full damage on creeps. Handy for securing ranged creep last hits.
  • If your teammates have a Force Staff, you can try proposing the idea for them to offensively Force Staff an enemy who is caught by your Aether Remnant. You can pull an enemy pretty far out of position between the Remnant pull and a follow-up Force Staff.
  • Only allies can see which Dissimilate portal you're targeting, not enemies. Stop randomly dancing the circles, you're not fooling anyone.
  • The formation of the Dissimilate portals is determined by putting one portal directly in front of Void Spirit on cast, and making the rest fill out the circle.
  • Your hero is always 'located' in the center Dissimilate portal until the spell ends. You can use this to grab runes or Aegis while banished in the Dissimilate as long as what you're picking up is near the middle of your center portal.
  • You can use Dissimilate to travel up and down cliffs.
  • Dissimilate just straight up ignores the silence from Silencer's Last Word.
  • Resonant Pulse only reduces damage from right clicks or instant attacks, not physical spell damage.
  • Resonant Pulse will absorb damage and won't trigger on-damage effects on your hero. This means that Salves, Clarities, Bottle, Urn, Blink Dagger, and sleeps won't get broken by attacks until the shield expires. If you need even more time, use Dissimilate's banish period to sneak in more regen.
  • Recasting Resonant Pulse does not stack the shield, the new cast will just refresh the duration and reset the buff values.
  • Resonant Pulse counts clones, such as Meepos or Arc Warden's Tempest Double, as heroes.
  • Pay attention to the Resonant Pulse visuals when you're hunting an invisible or fogged enemy. If it connects on a hero, you'll see particles coming from their direction.
  • Astral Step's instant attack can proc any attack modifiers your hero has, except cleave. It also has true strike and ignores disarms.
  • Astral Step fully affects spell immune enemies.
  • Astral Step can kill enemy couriers.
  • Multiple Astral Steps fully stack. If you need the damage, you can quickly use charges to go in and out on an enemy.

Counters

  • Hypnotizes, like Aether Remnant, can be dispelled with basic dispels, like putting a Lotus Orb on an ally.
  • The circles of Dissimilate are accurate to the damage AoEs. If you stand in the middle of a group of portals, Void Spirit won't be able to damage you.
  • You can purge the Resonant Pulse shield off Void Spirit.
  • Void Spirit cannot cast Dissimilate or Astral Step while rooted/leashed.
  • Dispelling the Astral Step debuff off your hero will just make the damage trigger immediately.
