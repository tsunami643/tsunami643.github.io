Viper

Tips

  • Nethertoxin Breaks enemy hero passives.
  • Viper looks funny with high attack speed.
  • That's really all there is to know about Viper...

Counters

  • You want to avoid putting DoTs on Viper. Every time he takes damage, Corrosive Skin will refresh on your hero if you're in 1400 range. Seeing as how Corrosive Skin is also giving him magic resistance, he'll typically take less damage than you from any DoT.
  • THIS INCLUDES RADIANCE. OH GOD PLEASE AVOID BUILDING A RADIANCE AGAINST VIPER.
  • Rubick and Morphling have the potential to use Viper's own Nethertoxin against himself to Break the Corrosive Skin passive.
