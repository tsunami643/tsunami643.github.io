Huskar

Tips

  • Because you don't have mana thanks to your Blood Magic innate, certain spells work weird on you. For example, Clockwerk's Power Cogs won't touch you, Lion can't Mana Drain you, and Nyx Assassin can't target you with Mind Flare.
    • You technically still have "mana" as shown by your INT value if you hover over your stats. For the purposes of spells that do math with your mana, you're effectively always at that max mana value.
  • Inner Fire is not a stun and does not cancel channels, it just applies knockback on enemies.
    • However, the knockback mechanic does move enemies, making it cancel things like Magnus's Skewer, Phoenix's Icarus Dive, Primal Beast's Onslaught, Spirit Breaker's Charge, Techies' Blast Off, Templar Assassin's Meld, and enemies in Shadow Amulet.
  • Agh's Shard Inner Fire gives you the full hero heal value on illusions.
  • You can cast spells (like Inner Fire) or items while mid-flight during Life Break.
  • Manually casting Burning Spears on enemy heroes won't draw creep aggro.
  • If you level up Burning Spears but keep building an old Spear stack debuff on an enemy, it'll continue using the lower level spell values until the debuff times out.
  • Burning Spears can't be dispelled.
  • Life Break dispels yourself on cast.
  • Huskar is debuff immune while flying in Life Break.
  • Agh's Scepter Life Break can taunt enemies through debuff immunity/BKB.
  • Turn on your Armlet after you Life Break, not before, to lose less HP. If you intentionally want less HP, do the opposite.
  • There is a guaranteed reliable way to take Rosh safely with just an Armlet no matter how bad your Rosh bash RNG is. Every time your Armlet is off cooldown, toggle it immediately after Rosh lands an attack and you should always be able to survive.
  • You can toggle your Armlet while TPing.
  • Remember to turn on Armlet before picking up Shield or Illusion runes.

Counters

  • Remember, none of Huskar's spells can cancel TPs. If you see this roided up madman leaping towards you, just start TPing out.
  • Breaks, like from Silver Edge or Shadow Demon's Agh's Scepter Demonic Purge, will disable Huskar's Berserker's Blood passive.
    • Viper is good at dealing with Huskar since Viper Strike Breaks Berserker's Blood and Corrosive Skin loves Burning Spears.
  • Life Break dispels Huskar on cast, but nothing can dispel the disarm from Heaven's Halberd.
  • Spirit Vessel can mitigate a lot of the regen he gets from Berserker's Blood at low HP. Bear in mind that Life Break dispels Huskar on cast, however.
  • Skadi and Shiva's can also reduce HP regen.
  • Ancient Apparition can make life hard for Huskar since Ice Blast prevents Berserker's Blood and lifesteal from healing and because Huskar gets very close to Shatter-range when he wants to max out Berserker's Blood.
  • Doom's Doom prevents any kind of healing.
  • Burning Spears can't be dispelled.
  • Spell reflection from Lotus Orb will reflect Life Break on Huskar's impact, not on the Life Break cast.
