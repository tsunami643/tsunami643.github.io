Huskar

Tips

  • Inner Fire does not cancel channels, it just moves enemies.
  • Inner Fire disarm works on Rosh.
  • You can cast Inner Fire while mid-flight during Life Break.
  • Burning Spears can't be dispelled.
  • Turn on your Armlet after you Life Break, not before, to lose less HP.
  • Spell immunity will block the self-damage from Life Break.
  • Life Break dispels yourself on cast.
  • Huskar is invulnerable while flying in Life Break.
  • Life Break works fully on Roshan.

Counters

  • Viper is good at dealing with Huskar since Nethertoxin breaks Berserker's Blood and Corrosive Skin loves Burning Spears.
  • Other Breaks like Silver Edge or Nyx's Vendetta will also disable his Berserker's Blood passive.
  • Life Break dispels Huskar on cast, but nothing can purge off the disarm from Heaven's Halberd.
  • Spirit Vessel can mitigate a lot of the regen he gets from Berserker's Blood at low HP. Bear in mind that Life Break dispels Huskar on cast, however.
  • Ancient Apparition can make life hard for Huskars since Ice Blast prevents Berserker's Blood and lifesteal from healing and because Huskar gets very close to Shatter-range when he wants to max out Berserker's Blood.
  • Burning Spears can't be dispelled.
  • Spell reflection from Lotus Orb or Anti-Mage's Counterspell will reflect Life Break on Huskar's impact, not on the Life Break cast.
