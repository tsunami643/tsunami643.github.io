Necrophos

Tips

  • "Nah he's dead, Death Pulse will kill him." HAHAHAHA, no. Death Pulse never gets them. Somehow people will always heal just barely enough HP just in time that the skull won't kill them. Always finish the kill either with Heartstopper Aura, Reaper's Scythe, or auto attacks.
  • Death Pulse can hit invis and fogged units.
  • You get Heartstopper regen stacks off of any unit you kill including buildings, wards, and creep denies. The only exceptions are illusions or the Tempest Double.
  • Ghost Shroud boosts HP/mana regen given to you from all sources, including teammates. Shroud yourself before a Keeper of the Light Chakra Magic or Omniknight Purification or Mekansm/Greaves or Arcane Boots.
  • Also, Shroud yourself before using a Magic Wand (which is absolutely core on Necro because of Ghost Shroud).
  • Ghost Shroud makes you immune to physical damage even from spells, such as Jugg's Omnislash or Bristleback's Quill Spray.
  • You get permanent Reaper's Scythe stacks off of Aegis kills or Wraith King Reincarnation kills.
  • Agh's Shard Death Seeker can be a useful spell to break Linken's Sphere so you can Reaper's Scythe a target.

Counters

  • You can't disjoint Death Pulse, so don't bother trying.
  • Glimmer Cape absolutely obliterates Necro. Save it for when he's Scything a target and then be amazed at how little damage the Scythe does.
  • Dazzle's Shallow Grave, Oracle's Fate's Edict and False Promise, and heroes that can banish a Scythed target like Shadow Demon, Outworld Destroyer, and Tusk are also good counters. Also, a well-timed Bane Nightmare.
  • You can potentially use Wind Waker on an ally being Scythed to save them.
  • Ghost Shroud can be purged the same ways as Ghost Scepter (Nullifier, Oracle's Fortune's End, Shadow Demon's Demonic Purge, Enchantress's Enchant, etc.)
  • Necro is taking bonus magic damage whenever he's in Ghost Shroud, so try to bait it out before tossing high magic damage nukes.
  • Spirit Vessel can prevent him from healing as much. As do Skadi and Shiva's.
  • Bane's Enfeeble reduces health restoration. Ancient Apparition's Ice Blast completely negates health restoration.
  • Necro loves playing against heroes who frequently give Stick charges, especially in lane. Try casting spells from fog since Magic Stick only gives charges if the spell caster is in vision.
