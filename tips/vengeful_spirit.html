Vengeful Spirit

Tips

  • Wave of Terror is a useful spell for scouting out dangerous territory because of its massive range+vision.
  • Wave of Terror damage can cancel consumable regen like Salves or Clarities from long range. It's also useful at breaking Blink Daggers from long range.
  • Wave of Terror works on Roshan.
  • When you're going for a sneaky Rosh, try to use Wave at such an angle that it won't show up under potential enemy vision.
  • Agh's Shard Wave of Terror reduces damage on every unit it hits. However, Venge only steals damage and armor from heroes and clones.
  • Vlad's can be a worthwhile pickup on VS because between Vengeance+Vladmir's Aura you're a huge bonus damage providing hero.
  • Vengeance Aura boosts damage on player controlled units.
  • When you die with Agh's Scepter, your Vengeance illusion spawns with its spell cooldowns immediately available.
  • Your Agh's Scepter Vengeance illusion may be a 'strong' illusion, but it's still an illusion. This means it deals reduced damage versus buildings and Roshan, it can't copy a lot of attack modifiers, it doesn't benefit from + raw armor items, and it doesn't benefit from + raw damage items. You don't even get the benefit of Vengeance Aura damage because Vengeance Aura provides + green damage.
  • Nether Swap goes through spell immunity, invulnerability, and sleeps.
  • Being able to Nether Swap invulnerable units means you can Swap heroes that are cycloned or things like Storm Spirits inside Ball Lightning.
  • If you're ever against an Enigma, your assignment is to sit around and play with your hair until you can use Swap to break Black Hole.
  • If things are getting chaotic in a fight, know that you can target spells on allies like Nether Swap by clicking on the allied hero's portrait in the top bar. You can't target enemies using topbar portraits, however.
  • Glimmer Cape can be a powerful pickup on VS since you can invisibly get into position to Swap, Swap a hero, and then fade out again.
  • Aether Lens can be incredibly valuable for boosting Nether Swap's range.

Counters

  • Wave of Terror is basically an AoE Medallion once maxed. This means that it's very easy for VS's team to take Rosh early and it means that tower diving a VS can be very dangerous.
  • During tier 3 tower defenses, make sure that the enemy team doesn't have wards on your high ground otherwise someone is almost certainly going to get Swapped to the low ground.
  • Vengeance Aura is granted by illusions of VS. Shadow Demon, Terrorblade, and Dark Seer can take advantage of this.
  • Vengeance Aura can be disabled by Breaks.
  • Venge's Agh's Scepter death illusion is considered a 'strong' illusion and cannot be instagibbed by things like Lion's Mana Drain, hexes, or Dagon.
  • Brewmaster's Primal Split Storm Panda can deal a lot of damage to illusions, even 'strong' illusions, with the Dispel Magic spell.
