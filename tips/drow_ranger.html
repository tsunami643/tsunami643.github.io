Drow Ranger

Tips

  • While Drow's Precision Aura innate does add a buff indicator to creep-heroes (like Visage's Familiars), they can't benefit from the AGI in any way.
  • Manually casting Frost Arrows on enemy heroes won't draw creep aggro.
  • The knockback on Gust doesn't break item channels, so don't bother using it to cancel a TP.
    • However, the knockback mechanic does move enemies, making it cancel things like Magnus's Skewer, Phoenix's Icarus Dive, Primal Beast's Onslaught, Spirit Breaker's Charge, Techies' Blast Off, and Templar Assassin's Meld.
  • Gust reveals invisible units for the duration of the silence.
  • Beware that Gust knockback can potentially push enemies out of a teammate Disruptor's Kinetic Field or Slark's Pounce leash.
  • Multishot gives a small amount of flying vision over the projectiles.
  • Multishot scales off your base damage. It does not multiply 'green' +dmg.
  • Despite its appearance, Multishot is not an instant attack. Therefore, the arrows do not apply attack modifiers (other than Frost Arrows), the spell won't break enemy Tranquil Boots, and it won't remove a Smoke buff off your hero if an attack lands on a target.
  • You can move while mid-Multishot, but Drow will continue aiming all Multishot arrows at the exact point you targeted.
    • So if you target Multishot far away, you can move your hero a lot without changing the arrows' angle significantly.
    • Alternatively, if you cast Multishot near your feet, you have a ton of control over the arrows' angle as you move.
  • You can use items while casting Multishot. It even allows you to cast items in any direction without having to turn.
  • You can cancel Multishot by hitting the 'Cancel Current Action'/stop key.
  • You can activate Agh's Shard Glacier while in the middle of casting Multishot and it won't cancel it.
  • Casting Agh's Shard Glacier immediately bumps away all enemy heroes that are standing on top of you.
  • All ranged allies benefit from the bonus attack range when standing on Agh's Shard Glacier. This includes creeps, like siege creeps.
  • Agh's Shard Glacier basically gives Drow a really short duration free pathing buff similar to Spectre's Spectral Dagger. The intention is to let you walk off the front of the Glacier, but if you line the Glacier up with a cliff, you can walk over walls too.
  • Standing on Agh's Shard Glacier is like standing on two 'levels' of high ground in terms of how vision works. To get vision of you, the enemy would need to have a ward or hero that's two ramps/stairs higher than you, similar to seeing a Monkey King that's perched on a tree.
    • Be aware attacking any enemies always gives vision of the attacker. So if you're using Glacier to hide/escape, don't attack anything.
  • Marksmanship procs have true strike. Not only will the Marksmanship damage go through, but so does your right click damage. This provides a built-in counter to evasion heroes.
  • Enemies will often try to get right on top of you to disable your Marksmanship passive. It's worth moving away to get it back rather than fighting at point blank range.
  • You can use Marksmanship's particle effect disappearing off your hero as a jank way to detect if invis heroes are hugging you.
  • Nullifier can dispel the Force Staff buff off heroes. However, Hurricane Pike still kind of works. If you're trying to escape while Nullified, you'll be able to create more distance by Piking a pursuing enemy away rather than using Pike as a self-Force Staff.

Counters

  • Get in her face. There is nothing Drows hate more than someone in melee range of her. She loses the bonuses from Marksmanship if enemy heroes are close to her, so try closing the distance even if you're a ranged hero.
    • Illusions won't disable Marksmanship, only actual heroes.
  • Gust reveals invisible units for the duration of the silence.
  • Marksmanship procs bypass your hero's main armor, but not bonus armor. This means that armor gained from agility won't help you very much against her, but raw armor items can be quite useful.
  • Evasion isn't too useful against Drow Ranger because Marksmanship gives her built-in accuracy.
  • Agh's Scepter Hypothermia stacks can be dispelled.
