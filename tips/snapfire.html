Snapfire

Tips

  • Firesnap Cookie works on yourself or allies when rooted. It also works on spell immune allies.
  • Firesnap Cookie can't target allies who are channeling spells or items. The Cookie also has no effect on banished allies.
  • Allies can't disjoint the Firesnap Cookie if they blink, it'll follow them everywhere. Grandma's gotta feed you.
  • Casting Firesnap Cookie on allies will interrupt any cast animations they're in the middle of.
  • Firesnap Cookie's bounce allows targets to pass over cliffs or Mars's Arena walls.
  • Landing after Firesnap Cookie destroys trees in a small radius around the target.
  • Consider tossing Firesnap Cookie on creeps to get stuns on enemy heroes. Creeps have more predictable pathing than your allied heroes do and enemies may not think to retreat from a creep wave.
  • Lil' Shredder attacks can apply any attack modifiers your hero has.
  • Each Lil' Shredder attack counts as a hero attack against ward units. This is very useful against heroes like Phoenix with Supernova, Shadow Shaman with Serpent Wards, Undying with Tombstone, Pugna with Nether Ward, or Zeus with Nimbus.
  • Each Lil' Shredder attack refreshes the armor reduction debuff on a target. To get the most value out of this, you theoretically want to shoot your first 5 attacks to get the most minus armor on a target, then apply the final attack right before the Shredder debuff times out on the target. This is mainly useful against towers when your whole team is pushing together.
  • Mortimer Kisses projectiles give vision as they fly and lingering vision wherever they land.
  • You can cancel Mortimer Kisses early by issuing a stop command.
  • Casting any items during Mortimer Kisses will cancel the spell.
  • If you Gobble Up a Pangolier in Rolling Thunder, he'll stun everything on the way to the target location.
  • You can Gobble Up enemy or neutral creeps, including enemy summons (but not creep-heroes). However, Spit Out counts as a deny, so you don't get any gold bounty from Gobbling units.
  • The cast range on Spit Out is the same as the cast range on Mortimer Kisses.
  • Respect the z-axis when casting Spit Out. If your Spit trajectory sends a hero over a cliff, they may end up landing on the cliff instead of the targeted AoE.

Counters

  • Both the Lil' Shredder buff on Snapfire and the minus armor debuff can be removed with basic dispels/purges.
  • Despite being forced movement, Firesnap Cookie will not take enemies out of Disruptor's Kinetic Field or Slark's Pounce. It also has no effect on heroes who are under Enigma's Black Hole or Faceless Void's Chronosphere.
  • Most types of disables will cancel the Mortimer Kisses cast. Some exceptions that don't cancel the cast: Disruptor's Glimpse, Vengeful Spirit's Nether Swap, roots/leashes, and taunts.
  • One notable long range Kiss-cancel is Sniper's Assassinate. Get vision with Shrapnel, then Assassinate her to end her Kiss cast.
  • Nyx Assassin can walk into any glob puddle, activate Spiked Carapace, and instantly cancel the Mortimer Kisses cast.
  • There is a minimum allowed range on Kiss targeting. If you walk into melee range of Snapfire, she can't get any Kisses on you.
