Enigma

Tips

  • Converting allied creeps into Eidolons counts as a deny.
  • You can make Eidolons out of Eidolons, thus further Horcruxing them. This information is highly relevant during Rosh attempts.
  • Jungle creeps have a tendency to use their spells whenever three units are in close proximity to them. If you just Converted something, chances are your Eidolons will eat the damage, but if you're moving to a new camp with old Eidolons, spread them out so they don't trigger a spell.
  • You can avoid triggering a neutral spell by dragging the camp out and Converting when the creeps are walking back to their camp.
  • When jungling, be aware that you can drop neutral aggro off of your Eidolons the same way you can drop tower aggro. Select the Eidolon in question and then a+click another allied unit and the creeps should lose interest in that Eidolon.
  • Attacks on buildings or allied units do not increase the attack counter for Eidolon multiplication.
  • However, if Eidolons only need one more attack before they multiply, a tower attack will work. I don't know why.
  • You can convert enemy siege creeps into Eidolons to help speed up pushes.
  • Midnight Pulse breaks trees in a massive AoE. Use this to counter Nature's Prophet's Sprout, burn down a bunch of trees during a chase, stun a Tree Dancing Monkey King, or to be super sneaky and carve a path to hide so you can easily Blink in for huge Black Holes.
  • Despite being magical damage, Midnight Pulse damage goes through spell immunity.
  • When going for solo kills with Black Hole in the early game, try to get a few multiplied Eidolons out first to make sure you have enough damage for a kill.
