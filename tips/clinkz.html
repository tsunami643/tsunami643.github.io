Clinkz

Tips

  • Death Pact cannot be used on allied units, but can be used on your Burning Army skeletons.
  • The armor reduction on hit with Death Pact works on buildings.
  • Searing Arrows affects structures. This + Skeleton Walk attack speed + Death Pact armor reduction makes Clinkz a crazy good pusher.
  • Similar to most other invis skills, Skeleton Walk has a very obvious cast animation (even if you cast it while invis). If you're hunting someone, run into the fog to refresh it if needed. Otherwise your target will see a puff of fire right next to them and bail.
  • Also like other invis skills, you can cast Skeleton Walk while channeling a TP.
  • Try to time your ganks so that if things go wrong, Skeleton Walk is off cooldown.
  • Burning Army attack damage is equivalent to a percentage Clinkz's own right click damage. The skeletons benefit from attack range buffs, Searing Arrows damage, and the "Searing Arrows Multishot" talent.
  • Burning Army skeletons won't attack Rosh and Rosh won't attack them.
  • Clinkz, Pangolier, and Io are the only heroes in the game that don't have to turn to cast items.

Counters

  • Dust is somewhat useful against Clinkz, but sentries are much better. Clinkz gets bonus move speed when he's Skelly Walkin', which makes him hard to catch with or without detection. The goal is to catch him off guard and take the offensive.
  • Consider keeping your own sentries if you're a carry and Clinkz is starting to roam. You can't depend on your supports to follow you around constantly with detection.
  • Get a Ghost Scepter if you're a support. It will save your life many a time.
  • Skeleton Walk attack speed can be dispelled off Clinkz.
  • It only takes two hero attacks to kill each Burning Army skeleton.
  • Damage block does not mitigate damage from Burning Army skeletons since the skeletons are classified as ward-type units and damage block doesn't work on wards.
