Zeus

Tips

  • When playing Zeus, check to see if your lane enemy has a Magic Stick. If you're mid and they do, try to exclusively cast Arc Lightning from the high ground. Enemies only get Stick charges if the caster is in vision. If they get a charge even when you think you weren't in vision, that means they have a ward that can see you.
    • Also check to see if the enemy has Tranquil Boots. Tranquil Boots don't break on spell damage, they only break on attacks. If you're wasting Arc Lightning casts on a target with Tranquils, they can heal it back fairly easily.
  • Arc Lightning won't bounce to targets that are in fog. Important to note in the mid lane if you're trying to get bounces to units that may be out of vision on high ground like a ranged creep.
  • Lightning Bolt gives vision, Arc Lightning does not. Cast Lightning Bolt first if you're chasing someone to make sure you don't lose your target in fog.
  • If you think someone is just about to escape vision, don't risk targeting Lightning Bolt directly on the hero, just cast it on the ground near them. Zeus will cancel his animation if it's directly targeted on a hero who goes into fog.
  • Ground targeting "adds" a bonus ~325 cast range to Lightning Bolt thanks to the search radius.
  • If you ground target Lightning Bolt from fog, you won't reveal yourself to the enemy who gets hit by it. If you directly target them, they'll get vision of you.
    • This is true for any spell that has the option between ground and direct unit targeting. If you ever directly target an enemy unit, the enemy team will briefly get vision of your hero. If you ground target, you won't be revealed through fog.
  • Early game you don't really have the mana to burn to use Lightning Bolt's true sight for dewarding unless you're 100% sure you know where a ward is. Late game, though, you should be casting it all over the place to counterward.
  • Lightning Bolt has a ministun which allows you to cancel channels.
  • Heavenly Jump allows you to pass over cliffs and can even get you out of Disruptor's Kinetic Field which most other movement spells get stopped by.
  • Try to maintain mana for your Thundergod's Wrath in the early game. The potential of getting a kill on a hero from across the map will probably be more useful than the 2 last hits you picked up with Arc Lightning.
  • In late game situations, Thundergod's Wrath's biggest asset is the instant global vision it gives of the entire enemy team. Don't hesitate to cast it during downtime to verify enemy positions or check if they may have Smoked or use it as soon as a team fight starts to get every enemy's location.
    • You can use Thundergod's Wrath as temporary counter-initiation against teams with a lot of Blink Daggers.
  • Consider popping Thundergod's Wrath immediately after seeing an enemy Tormentor notification. You may be able to finish off some low HP stragglers.
  • Nimbus will Lightning Bolt and reveal invis units that walk into the field. It will also shock enemies under Smoke, but will not reveal them.
  • Enemies need vision of the Nimbus unit to right click it, even after it Lightning Bolts them. Try to place it on cliffs or above trees whenever possible.
  • The Nimbus will always attack the unit closest to the center of the field.
  • The Nimbus works normally in a Faceless Void Chronosphere.

Counters

  • Arc Lightning can only bounce to you if Zeus has vision of you.
  • Get a Magic Stick if he's spamming Arc Lightning. Just remember that you only get stick charges on spells if the enemy is in vision.
  • Buy a Smoke of Deceit against Zeus. Thundergod's Wrath only reveals invisible units, but does not damage them. But even then, Smoke is a weird type of invis, so even a Refreshed Thundergod's Wrath wouldn't kill you. Buy a Smoke. Use it when escaping with low HP. Laugh at Zeus in all chat.
    • That being said, Thundergod's Wrath is often used as a scouting tool in the late game. If your team is smoked and Zeus casts ult, fog will be revealed around you and your teammates showing your general location, he just won't be able to see your hero models.
  • Your team should always prioritize building some sources of magic resistance against Zeus. Be it in the form of Cloaks, Glimmer Capes, BKBs, or Pipe.
  • Melee heroes deal twice as much damage to the Nimbus compared to ranged heroes.
