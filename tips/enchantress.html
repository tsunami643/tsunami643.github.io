Enchantress

Tips

  • Impetus calculates the damage by the distance between you and the target once the projectile hits the target. There's a cap at 1750 distance.
  • You can Enchant nearly every enemy/neutral unit. Necronomicon creeps and enemy illusions are both A+++ steals.
  • Only one unit can be Enchanted at a time.
  • Enchanted lane creeps will benefit from the Glyph invulnerability.
  • Casting Enchant on enemies will dispel/purge them. Here are some buffs worth noting that can be purged: Aphotic Shield, Surge, Ion Shell, Flame Guard, Empower, Jingu Mastery, Overpower, Ghost Scepter, Crimson Guard's active, and Haste/DD/Arcane/Regen (but not Invis) runes.
  • Untouchable makes you a great hero to tank Rosh since he attacks in bullet time.

Counters

  • If you're a right clicker, try to let your supports take care of Bambi. You'll be wasting your time trying to kill her mid game when nukes will touch her Untouchable fluffy tail quicker than you can.
  • You can "store" the max attack speed second hit of an Echo Sabre by cancelling the attack before it connects. The attack speed buff lasts indefinitely, so you can spend the first right click on a creep and then use the Echo Sabre buff to go through Untouchable whenever you attack Enchantress.
  • Spirit Vessel will significantly reduce the HP she gains from Nature's Attendants.
